Use: The game ends when one of the players reaches 5 points.
 Left paddle:
<ul>
    <li>'z' raises the left paddle</li>
    <li>'x' moves the left paddle down</li>
</ul>
Right paddle:
<ul>
    <li>'i' raises the right paddle</li>
    <li>'n' moves the right paddle down</li>
</ul>
Other commands:
<ul>
    <li>'ECHAP' to exit the game</li>
    <li>'SPACE' pauses the game</li>
</ul>
